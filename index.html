Caring Friends Cat Rescue

We rescue and rehabilitate as many abandoned and neglected cats as we can each year. Once healthy, we find loving, safe and permanent homes for all of the cats that we take in to our rescue. We have TNR projects at two separate feral colonies where we feed, give fresh water to and monitor at least 100 feral cats each day. We are an all volunteer no-kill 501(c)3 non-profit cat rescue.

Adoptable Cats

All adoptions require an adoption application, contract, interview and a donation. Many of our cats live in foster homes and do not attend every adoption event. If there is a cat you would like to meet, please email us prior to the weekend so that we can bring the cat to the adoption event.

Donatations

All donations are GREATLY appreciated by our cats. No matter how large or small every donation makes a huge difference to our cats. Here is a list of items that we use frequently and are part of our wish list for donations: Bedding, Dry Cat Food, Canned Cat Food, Toys, easy to sanitize, Scoopable/Clumping Cat Litter, Money for vet bills, Crates/Cages, TruCatch Heart of the Earth humane traps, Cat Trees/Scratching Posts,etc.
